Self-Assessment Capability Survey Form (SACS)

In order to address the numerous IAEA Member States’ requests for assistance with their national infrastructure for responding to nuclear security events (NSE), the IAEA has developed a new assistance programme entitled “IAEA Assistance programme on Nuclear Security Response Capabilities” (Program). A Member State taking part in the Programme is on the list of the States that requested such support from IAEA.

The Programme consists of three phases (Planning Phase, Implementing Phase, Sustaining Phase). This application is an instrument developed by IAEA in order to help its Member States to complete the objective of the first - Planning Phase. This application is designed to enable participating States to i) identify areas for improvement in existing States’ nuclear security response capabilities and ii) to develop a comprehensive plan of actions, Capability Development Plan (CDP) to address any nuclear security response capability which has been identified for improvement.

Once such a CDP is developed and nationally endorsed, a second phase of the Programme, the implementation phase, can be initiated. The existence of the approved CDP will ensure provision of IAEA assistance and support therein identified.
